Antti Hietanen
Antti hietanen received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the Tampere University of Technology, Finland, in 2015. He is currently pursuing the M.Sc. degree as a member of the Computer Vision Group at the Department of Signal Processing. His current studies focus on Signal Processing and Pervasive Systems.

Jukka Lankinen
Jukka Lankinen is with the Department of Signal Processing, Tampere University of Technology. He received his M.Sc. in 2009 and his Ph.D. in 2014 from Lappeenranta University of Technology. His research interests are local part detectors and descriptors and video processing.
